Food

You just got off a long, long, long flight, and what's the first thing that comes to mind...FOOD! I know right. But where to start, Japan has so many options. Well I am here to help you. The answer is Ramen, the crown jewel of japanese cusine. You will have so many options, however, bear in mind, Ramen is the japanese equivalent of pizza. What do I mean with that, well toppings are paid separetly. Of you ask just for Ramen, they will bring a plain Ramen, almost no toppings, such as eggs, noriweed, vegetables... Sounds unveliable but it is true. That same thing happen to me on my first night in Tokyo. I ordered ramen and to my dissapointment, almost no toppings, I could not understand why! But a lovely lady seating next to me pointed at the restaurant wall, where all the toppings where listed alongside prices. The more you add, the more the ramen costs. See, just like our pizza. So my tip is always ask for your ramen toppings belive me, it will make such a difference.
